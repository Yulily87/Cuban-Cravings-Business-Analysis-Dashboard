# Cuban-Cravings-Business-Analysis-Dashboard

Food Truck Business Analytics

This project analyzes sales and customer data for Cuban Cravings, a food truck specializing in authentic Cuban cuisine.

Business Insight

- Cuban Sandwich and Pork Bowl are the top-selling main dishes.
  
- Appetizer "Papas Rellenas" and sides like Plantains, Tostones, and Yuca Frita increase average order value.
  
- Drinks contribute steadily to total revenue.
  
- Repeat customers contribute significantly to revenue.
  
- Sales peak on weekends and event days, useful for inventory and staffing planning.
